# Symphony - Comprehensive Composition for Kubernetes

<p align="center">
<img src="./assets/symphony-logo.svg" width="200" >
</p>

Symphony is a Kubernetes-native controller designed to orchestrate the
composition of resources in a declarative and intuitive manner. Drawing
inspiration from industry proven solutions like and AWS
CloudFormation, Symphony sets out to simplify the management of complex
infrastructure within kubernetes by offering a more flexible and
user-friendly experience.
